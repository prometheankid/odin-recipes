Protein Eggs

Description

The first meal of the day assuming you have just hit a 4:00am workout will be your protein filled breakfast along with some carbs. Eggs are one of the most budjet friendly and protein rich sources.HOwever, gym food must not be bland and boring.This recipe mixes health and taste, and presents a recipe you can always look forward to after your early battle had been won

The aim of my Gym Recipes is to make meals as enjoyable and flavourfull as possible whilst making sure the amount of time spent cooking meals is kept to a minimum.

Ingridients

  • 5 Eggs
  • 1/2 a can of HUmmus
  • 2 Salad Tomatoes
  • Blackpepper
  • 2 slices Brown Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Green Chilli
  • Salt

Steps

Method 1

  1. Boil your Eggs
  2. Toast your Bread to your liking
  3. While waiting for eggs to boil, dice your tomatoes in to cubes
  4. Spread peanut butter on your toasted Bread
  5. When Eggs are ready, peel and slice each egg in to halfs
  6. Put your eggs and chopped tomatoes on to your bread as shown in picture
  7. Add Black pepper,salt and finely diced green chillies to your liking
  8. Indulge

Mehod 2

Follow the above preperation Method, However, Before indulging mix everything up, apart from bread and peanut butter as follows

  1. Add peanut butter on to your slices
  2. Dice eggs into cubes
  3. In a Bowl, add your chopped eggs, Tomatoes, and Green Chillies
  4. Add Hummus into the Bowl
  5. Add Black pepper and salt to your liking
  6. INDULGE, Take a bite from the bowl and the alternate it with your slices.

Do you yourself a favour and enjoy the confusion green chillies, peanut butter and Hummus brings to your taste buds. Breakfast Sorted.
